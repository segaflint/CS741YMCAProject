Profile

To view the information associated to your profile, click the "Profile" button on the header. 

Viewable information includes:

  • Your YMCA Program Registration App username
  • Your membership status
  • Your registered programs
  • If you are a YMCA Staff member, your staff status

Programs

To view and register for the offered YMCA programs, click the "Programs" button on the header

To find specific program, type the desired program name into the search bar.

Program information is shown for each offered program. 

To register for a program, click "Register" beneath the desired program. Upon signing in for your first session on location, you will be asked to pay a YMCA staff for your registration.

If you are registered for a specific program and would like not to be, click "Un-Register". To receive a refund for your registration, contact a YMCA staff.

YMCA Staff Only:

To un-register a specific user from a program click the trashcan icon next to the username you wish to un-register under "Registered Participants by username." You can also un-register a user from the "Users" page.

To add an offered program, click "Add Program" at the top of the Programs page. Then fill in the program information and click "Submit" to save the program.

To modify an existing program, click "Edit Program" under the existing program.

User Management (Staff use only)

To view user information, click the "Users" button on the header.

Viewable information includes:

  • A user's YMCA Program Registration App username
  • A user's membership status
  • A user's registered programs
  • A user's staff status

To change a user's membership status, toggle the "Not a YMCA member" or "YMCA member" button.

To delete a user, click the "Delete User" button on the desired user. NOTE: This simply inactivates the user from being able to access their account; All information related to the user is kept.
